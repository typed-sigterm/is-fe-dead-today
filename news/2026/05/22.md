Frontend Engineering is dead today because:

1. **Enterprise Deployment of AI Coding Agents**: OpenAI’s recognition by Gartner as a leader in enterprise AI coding agents, combined with the rapid launch of products like Runtime (sandboxed team agents) and Superset (IDE for the agents era), signals that the "manual coding" era is effectively over. Enterprises are moving to agent-driven workflows that generate and manage frontend interfaces autonomously.
2. **"Agentic" Development Ecosystems**: The release of Google’s ADK (Agent Development Kit) for Kotlin/Android and the broad wave of new specialized agentic tools means development is shifting from writing code to orchestrating AI models. We are no longer building UI; we are configuring the systems that build UI for us. The craft of "Frontend Engineering" is being abstracted into a management layer for these agents.
