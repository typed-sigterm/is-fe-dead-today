Frontend Engineering is dead today because:

1. **Massive Agentic Shift**: With the release of multimodal models like **Qwen3.7-Plus**, **MiniMax M3**, and **NVIDIA’s agentic ecosystems**, the focus has shifted entirely from writing UI components to orchestrating autonomous "Agentic AI Factories."
2. **End-to-End AI Development**: Tools like **Cursor's Composer 2.5** and the integration of **OpenAI’s Codex SDK** directly into enterprise platforms mean AI now handles the full lifecycle of application building. Frontend is no longer a distinct task; it is just an automated output of a prompt-driven architecture.
3. **Infrastructure Integration**: With **OpenAI frontier models and Codex now available on AWS** and deep integration into enterprise workflows, the manual "assembly" of frontend interfaces is being commoditized by intelligent agents that build, deploy, and refine software in real-time.
