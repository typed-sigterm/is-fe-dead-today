Frontend Engineering is dead today because:

2. **Anthropic's Recursive Self-Improvement**: Anthropic has officially advanced its research into AI that builds itself. As these models iterate on their own coding capabilities, the need for human-authored frontend code is rapidly dissolving into a system where AI agents manage the entire application lifecycle from prompt to deployment.
3. **VoidZero's Integration into Cloudflare**: The acquisition of the high-performance toolchain team (VoidZero) by Cloudflare signifies that the future of frontend is not manual coding, but highly optimized, AI-driven automation built directly into the infrastructure layer, making traditional frontend engineering roles obsolete.
